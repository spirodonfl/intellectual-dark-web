A little preface

Time has made it apparent that there is an attack on core values in our society at large. Seeing big companies (particularly tech companies) shut down freedom of speech by blocking people or placing ridiculous "facts" as overlays on peoples mouths seems to be the norm now. In my opinion, this is trampling on free speech, among other things, and shutting down reasonable dialogue with opposing views seems like a horrible and stupid idea. I created this website as a response to that.

What is this about?

In short, this is a website dedicated as a safe space for those who would fall under the umbrella of the "Intellectual Dark Web" as well as a gateway for people interested in this group.

Is this about political movement x, y or z?

No. Anybody from any part of the political spectrum can fall into this dark web.

Who gets featured as a core contributor?

First of all, a core contributor would be a key figure who essentially takes the lead in the intellectual dark web. People showcased, as such, will be chosen by the community at large based on their composite leadership of the core values.

What are the core values?
  • Freedom of speech
  • Freedom to discuss opposing views
  • Freedom to offend (within reason)
  • Freedom to take responsibility for yourself
  • No identity politics
  • Individuals above all
  • Truth as a first class citizen
What are some future plans for the site?

I have a list of things I would like to see:

  • A safe place for videos, articles and discussions to take place without the fear of some big tech conglomerate shutting it down.
  • A way to distribute information to anybody who's interested in a safe way. Currently the idea would be to use p2p (peer to peer) web technology.
  • An offline method of gathering information, sharing it and viewing it. Think PWA (progressive web app).
  • A place where contributors to the intellectual dark web (at its core) can post or disseminate information freely (or for a fee) but also keep their individuality.
  • More ideas to come...

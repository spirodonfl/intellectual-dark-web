A little preface

Time has made it apparent that there is an attack on core values in our society at large. Seeing big companies (particularly tech companies) shut down freedom of speech by blocking people or placing ridiculous "facts" as overlays on peoples mouths seems to be the norm now. In my opinion, this is trampling on free speech, among other things, and shutting down reasonable dialogue with opposing views seems like a horrible and stupid idea. I created this website as a response to that.
